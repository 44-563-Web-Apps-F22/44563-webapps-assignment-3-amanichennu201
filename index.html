Guntur Museum

Guntur Museum is located in Guntur District, India. There are only three departments in that museum. The first department is about how history of guntur. The king KrishnaDevaRayaruled that city and many books can be found in the museum about hisdynasty and his ruling. The second department is about Images of palace and its rootmap. The third department is about animals previously lived there.
